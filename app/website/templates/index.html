Welcome to CI/CD Diaries

CI/CD Diaries is a blog that aims to provide a comprehensive guide to Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment. The blog is a collection of articles that cover the basics of CI/CD, the tools used in CI/CD, and the best practices to follow while implementing CI/CD in your projects.

Whether you are a beginner looking to learn about CI/CD or an experienced developer looking to improve your CI/CD skills, CI/CD Diaries has something for everyone. The blog is updated regularly with new articles, so make sure to check back often for the latest content.

This is a student project, but with some effort, it has the potential to become a fully-fledged website.
